Unofficial MA (Estimated) Retroactive Payment Calculator

This tool can be used to calculate an estimate of the retroactive pay you may receive as a result of the signing of a new collective agreement on March 1st, 2024,
covering a period starting on October 1st, 2022 until the date that pay rate updates are expected to be effective in the pay system.

NOTE: This is not an official tool and is not affiliated with either PIPSC or the Government of Canada. Results may not reflect the actual amount of retroactive pay you will receive, due to information unique to your pay situation that isn't accounted for in this program,and potential errors in the program itself.

To use this tool, enter all applicable periods of employment and their associated dates, weekly hours, position and step.
If you would like to reflect multiple periods of employment at different levels, click "Add another period of pay to the table".
You may adjust the date that payments are calculated to be retroactive to based on estimates of when pay rate updates are expected to be effective in the pay system.
Periods of LWOP are not currently able to be be incorporated into this calculator. If you would like to reflect periods of LWOP, enter two (or more) periods of mployment with the end date of initial periods being on the day before your LWOP, and the start dates being on the day following your LWOP.
The fourth year of the collective agreement has also not been included in these calculations, as it is unlikely to be relevant.

When you have entered all applicable information and have verified it is correct, click "CALCULATE"
